Navigating High School at AMPS: A Roadmap to Success

High school is a pivotal phase in a student’s educational journey—shaping their aspirations, refining their skills, and preparing them for university and beyond. At Al Maaref Private School (AMPS), we offer a well-rounded high school experience that empowers students to thrive both academically and personally.

A World-Class American Curriculum

AMPS follows a rigorous American high school curriculum recognized globally for its emphasis on critical thinking, creativity, and flexibility. Students study core subjects such as:

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies

Electives allow students to explore specialized interests, from technology to the arts, while inquiry-based learning nurtures analytical and independent thinking.

Highly Qualified Faculty and Personalized Learning

Our dedicated team of experienced educators brings international expertise to every classroom. Teachers at AMPS not only instruct but also mentor and guide students through academic challenges and personal development.

With small class sizes, we offer tailored support—ensuring every student gets the attention and resources they need to thrive.

College and Career Readiness

Preparing students for university and life beyond school is a core mission. Our college counseling team supports students in:

  • Course selection aligned with university goals
  • Crafting compelling college applications
  • Exploring career pathways and global opportunities

Graduates from AMPS have been accepted into prestigious universities across the United States, Canada, UK, and beyond.

Holistic Development Beyond Academics

We believe learning should extend beyond textbooks. Our high school students have access to a wide range of extracurricular programs, including:

  • Debate clubs, robotics teams, and performing arts
  • Varsity sports and fitness initiatives
  • Leadership training and community service projects

These experiences cultivate confidence, teamwork, and resilience—qualities essential for success in higher education and life.

A Supportive and Inclusive Community

AMPS fosters a nurturing environment where students feel safe, included, and inspired to grow. Through character education, we instill values such as respect, empathy, and responsibility.

We also promote strong parent-school partnerships through regular communication, meetings, and events, ensuring a unified approach to student success.

Final Thoughts

High school is a transformative journey—and at Al Maaref Private School, we make sure it leads to meaningful growth and lasting success. With a globally respected curriculum, passionate educators, and a focus on holistic development, AMPS is proud to shape future-ready graduates.

If you’re seeking a school in Dubai that balances academic rigor with personal growth, AMPS is the destination. Join our vibrant learning community and help your child take the next step toward a bright, impactful future.
