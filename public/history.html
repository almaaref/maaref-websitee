About Maaref American School

Established in 1987, Maaref American school (Previously known as Maaref American School- AMPS) is a leading American curriculum school in Dubai, UAE, operating under the umbrella of First Education Holding.

First Education Holding is an education holding company established in 2006 and located in Bahrain.

  • FEH’s vision is to become a leading high-quality education company catering to the educational needs of families across the MENA region. Its mission is to grow by acquiring private K–12 schools and education-related businesses throughout the MENA region.
  • FEH provides strategic management support to its schools to optimize their growth and educational quality. At AMPS, we aim to provide a student-centered environment that encourages inquiry, exploration, critical thinking, and real-world skill transfer.
