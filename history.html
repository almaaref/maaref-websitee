About Al Maaref American School

Established in 1987, Al Maaref American School (previously known as Al Maaref Private School - AMPS) is a leading American curriculum school in Dubai, UAE, operating under the umbrella of First Education Holding.

First Education Holding (FEH) is an education holding company established in 2006 and located in Bahrain.

  • FEH’s vision is to become a leading high-quality education company catering to the educational needs of families across the MENA region. Its mission is to grow by acquiring private K–12 schools and education-related businesses throughout the MENA region.
  • FEH provides strategic management support to its schools to optimize their growth and educational quality. At MAS, FEH aims to provide a student-centered environment that encourages inquiry, exploration, critical thinking, and real-world skill transfer.
