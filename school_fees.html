Fee Policy and Payment Instructions

Admission Fee: AED 500 (non-refundable) is payable at the time of new registration. AED 250 will be charged on all bounced cheques.

For New Parents: A 10% deposit of the annual tuition fee must be paid within 7 days of receiving an offer of a place. This is non-refundable unless withdrawn per our Withdrawal Policy.

For Current Parents: A 5% deposit of the annual tuition fee or AED 500 (whichever is higher) is due at re-registration. Non-refundable unless withdrawn as per policy.

Remaining Payment: Payment is to be made via cheques – three post-dated cheques must be submitted upon acceptance or re-registration.

  • Books, uniform, transport and other fees are to be paid by cash or with the first installment cheque.
  • 1st Installment: 40% by cash/cheque dated 25th August 2024
  • 2nd Installment: 30% by cheque dated 7th December 2024
  • 3rd Installment: 30% by cheque dated 7th March 2025

All fees must be paid before the first day of school to ensure access to campus facilities.
