School Timings

Classes run from Sunday to Thursday:

  • KG Section: 7:30 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Elementary & Middle School: 7:30 AM – 2:30 PM
  • Fridays and holidays follow a special schedule.

Transportation

We provide safe and reliable transportation services across Sharjah and nearby areas. Parents can register at the front desk or contact our transport coordinator.

Uniform Guidelines

Uniforms promote unity and discipline. Students must follow the dress code strictly. Uniforms are available at our approved supplier outlet.

  • KG: Blue polo shirt with navy shorts/skirt
  • Grades 1–5: White shirt with navy pants/skirt
  • PE Days: Sports tracksuit with school logo
