A Community of Excellence and Inclusion

Al Maaref Private School is a kind, inclusive, tolerant community where diverse opportunity, personal development, high achievement and collaborative partnership define who we are, and where our values underpin all we do.

Respect

We nurture an atmosphere of mutual respect between all members of our community, fostering self-esteem, emotional maturity and lasting friendships in a positive, happy environment.

Scholarship

We encourage the growth of intellectual curiosity, independence and innovation within a scholarly environment supported by specialists who inspire lifelong learning.

Inclusivity

We remain loyal to our founding principles to educate students from all backgrounds, fostering diversity, acceptance, and a deep sense of belonging.

Integrity

We promote self-discipline, responsibility and uphold values like honesty, humility, and kindness.

Courage

We help students embrace challenges, take risks, and persevere with resilience and confidence.

Collaboration

We instill a spirit of cooperation, teamwork and mutual respect to make a difference in our community and world.
