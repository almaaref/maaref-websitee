Student Council at AMPS

At Maaref American School (AMPS), the Student Council serves as the official voice of the student body, representing ideas, opinions, and concerns to school leadership.

Its purpose is to foster leadership, responsibility, and active participation. The council meets regularly to plan and implement school-wide initiatives that promote student engagement and positive change.

Led by an elected President, Vice President, Treasurer, and Secretary—with Class Representatives across all grade levels—the council ensures every student has a voice.

  • Promote leadership and communication skills
  • Plan school events, service projects, and awareness campaigns
  • Encourage student input in decision-making
  • Support initiatives that strengthen school values

At AMPS, we believe in empowering students to take ownership of their educational journey—and the Student Council plays a vital role in that mission.
