Director's Message

Dear Parents, Students, and Visitors,

Welcome to Al Maaref American School. Since our establishment in 1995 as part of the First Education Holding group, we have been committed to nurturing our students’ academic, personal, and moral growth.

Built on a foundation of respect, integrity, and community, we embrace diversity, value every individual, and foster collaboration. Our American curriculum, accredited by NEASC, equips students with the skills, knowledge, and character they need to succeed in higher education and beyond.

At MAS, education goes beyond academics—we focus on developing resilience, responsibility, and life skills that prepare students for the future. Guided by our vision for excellence, we foster innovation in every aspect of learning and development, ensuring our students grow with confidence, adaptability, and purpose.

We invite you to join us in this journey of excellence as we continue shaping confident, capable, and compassionate leaders of tomorrow.
