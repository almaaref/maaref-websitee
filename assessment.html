Benchmarking and International Assessment

Al Maaref American School is committed to delivering a world-class education grounded in academic excellence, personalized learning, and international best practices. To monitor student progress and ensure continuous improvement, we engage in a comprehensive range of internal and external assessments. These assessments allow us to track attainment and growth, inform teaching and learning, and support university readiness and global diploma recognition.

International and Benchmarking Assessments Administered at MAS:

  • MAP Growth
  • SAT
  • NGRT
  • PSAT
  • CAT4
  • Advanced Placement (AP)
  • PISA
  • TIMSS
  • PIRLS

International and Benchmarking Assessments Administered at MAS:

  • Data is analysed regularly to inform instruction, intervention, and enrichment.
  • Class placements and grouping decisions are guided by a triangulation of assessment results.
  • Assessment outcomes support internal evaluation, inspection readiness, and school improvement planning.
  • High school graduation requirements are aligned with UAE Ministry of Education standards and international university admissions criteria.

University Recognition & Diploma Equivalency

The MAS high school diploma is recognized for equivalency by the UAE Ministry of Education. Combined with SAT and IELTS completion and, where applicable, AP coursework, our graduates are well-prepared for admission to top universities across:

  • The United Arab Emirates
  • United States and Canada
  • United Kingdom and Europe
  • MENA Region
  • Australia and Asia

Our assessment framework ensures that students graduate not only with strong academic credentials, but also with the qualifications, documentation, and confidence to pursue higher education anywhere in the world.
