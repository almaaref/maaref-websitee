School Advisory Board

Meet Our Advisory Board

The objective of the School Advisory Board (SAB) is to uphold the school vision and mission for the present and the future, work cooperatively and effectively with the AMPS administration for the improvement of the school.

The Role of School Advisory Board

The SAB provides strategic guidance and support on the school’s overall progress, advocates for all students, and is actively involved in school improvement and accreditation. The SAB demonstrates diversity in discussion and unity in action. Responsibilities include:

  • Steering the school and setting its strategic direction
  • Monitoring the school’s and principal’s performance
  • Managing the discretionary budget
  • Planning and approving policies
  • Overseeing the implementation of policies
  • Self-evaluation and improvement
  • Succession planning
